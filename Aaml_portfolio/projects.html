Green Marketing System

This website designed to help farmers to sale their fresh products using this website instead
of going to supermarkets and wait long times there and sale their products, but using this website every farmer
can share its own product with his information and location and people can go to his farm and buy from him whatever they
want, by this way farmers will save their time and their products from corruption in markets.
I use php,HTML, java script ,My SQL .

Roel Café

This site is designed to help Roel Café sell and display the products of a group of merchants using this site, where each
merchant can rent a shelf and display their products on the site. The site also contains
a map that helps customers to go to the store and buy what they want from it. In this way,
the seller will save his time in responding to inquiries , the customer will know the products available in the store, and he can communicate with merchants via the site.
I use php,HTML, java script ,My SQL .
